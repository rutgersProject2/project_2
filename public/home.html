What we do

Verify gives users a new way to share important information with their partners. It is our mission to simplify how dating health is managed. Using a verified network of medical professionals and our secure platform your sensitive information is safe and readily available. Remember, Verify is purely consentual...as it should be!

How to get started

Using Verify is simple! Visit your qualified medical professional and ask them to enter your information on the Verify platform. Once you have your unique ID, you can log in to your Verify account and see your most recent screening results. These results will link to your application on your smart device.

Use a simple app

Using the Verify app for smartphone is simple. Every user is issued a QR code within their unique app. Scanning another user's code will alert you as to whether or not that person is free of any STD or STIs. Any discrepency in their record? Verify let's you know in real time. Keep your dating health safe and worry free!
