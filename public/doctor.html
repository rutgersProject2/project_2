Verify helps you help your Patients

Create a New Patient

Using the Verify platform you can easily add a new patient record to your medical database. This record allows the patient to access and use the Verify website and application.

Update a Patient Record

Once a record is created with Verify, each patient is assigned a unique id. Using this id you can easily update their existing records with each new medical visit.

View Patient Records

Use the same unique id to view your patient's record. You can see their previous medical visit and bill of health for reference. Verify helps keep you and your patient on the same page!
